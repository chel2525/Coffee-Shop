CanCode Coffee Shop Product Details:
Coffee, Whole Bean, Medium-Dark Roast, Kona, is Grown on the fertile slopes of Kona's Hualalai Volcano. 100% pesticide free. Ever since Hawaii's first commercial coffee crops were planted in the cool, lush uplands of Kona in the 1840s, coffee has been a vibrant part of Kona's lifestyle and economy. Rich, robust CanCode coffee is now savored by gourmets around the world. At Cancode Coffee Shop, we pride ourselves on producing a smooth, full-bodied coffee without any bitter aftertaste. We roast our premium coffee in a small batches and do not warehouse any products, which ensures that your purchase is always fresh. Enjoy

Coffee Fun Fact:
The word coffee entered the English language in 1582 via the Dutch koffie, borrowed from the Ottoman Turkish kahve, borrowed in turn from the Arabic qahwah. The Arabic word qahwah was traditionally held to refer to a type of wine whose etymology is given by Arab lexicographers as deriving from the verb قَهِيَ qahiya, 'to lack hunger', in reference to the drink's reputation as an appetite suppressant.[citation needed] The terms coffee pot and coffee break originated in 1705 and 1952 respectively.
